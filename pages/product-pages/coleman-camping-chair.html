Our Review

After extensive testing in various outdoor settings, we found the Coleman Portable Camping Quad Chair to be an exceptional value for its price point. The chair strikes an excellent balance between comfort, durability, and practical features that enhance the outdoor experience.

The first thing we noticed was how easy it is to set up and take down. The chair unfolds in seconds with no assembly required, and collapses just as quickly when it's time to pack up. The included carrying bag with shoulder strap makes transportation simple, though at around 8 pounds, it's not the lightest camping chair available.

Comfort is where this chair truly shines. The padded seat and back provide excellent support, and the 18-inch seat height is ideal for most adults. We found we could sit comfortably for hours around the campfire without the discomfort that often comes with less padded camping chairs. The armrests are positioned at a natural height and provide good support.

The built-in 4-can cooler pouch is more than just a novelty featureâ€”it's genuinely useful. During our testing, it kept beverages cool for several hours, especially when the cans were already chilled. The mesh cup holder easily accommodates various drink containers, from water bottles to coffee mugs.

We were impressed with the chair's durability during our testing period. The steel frame feels robust, and the polyester fabric showed no signs of wear or fading despite exposure to sun, light rain, and regular use. The stitching is solid throughout, suggesting this chair will hold up well over multiple camping seasons.

One minor drawback is the side pocket, which is somewhat shallow. While useful for small items like a phone or sunglasses, larger items might not be secure. Additionally, while the chair performs well on most surfaces, on very soft ground (like sand), the legs can sink slightly.
